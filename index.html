About Me

Howdy! I am Cynax. You might know me by H20Ghost. Currently, I am a student who is very interested in music.

My projects includes a variety of genres such as techno, lo-fi, cinematic, house, and eceteras. Additionally, I sometimes experiments with other rather unconventional genres as well.

There are many great artists who I looked up to and inspired me such as Mameyudoufu, Kivamkii, Auvic, Zekk, Psyqui, Vaporcruise, and M2U.

While I am still pretty new to the music production scene, I've composed some recognizable pieces such as Alter, Jester, or Dismal with 11,000+, 4,000+, and 4,000+ listeners on Newgrounds respectively.

Regardless, I will continue to improve my skill as an artist and hoping to work with a rhythm game in the future!
